What is miniBUDDY ?

miniBUDDY is a safe platform for kids.This project is focused on keeping ancient tales of Grandmother for today's kids. we plan to build this platform by collecting stories with some beautiful moral speeches for the new generation. we hope and believe that users of this site will take it in a friendly way.

Our goal

Our goal is to deliver moral pieces of knowledge through this medium to the new generation. To keep our culture safe, to get a better society, we should develop ourselves, our next generation. Our community will provide each of the tales with a piece of good moral knowledge. The culture of hearing tales from our grandparents over the mid-night sky is adoreable. We will provide more stories later soon. Users can enjoy the cartoon animations for free.

Features

Tales

miniBUDDY will tell you very exciting tales with the categories of adventure, prince and princess, horror, ancient tales etc etc...! Users can upload their own tales, ideas, writtings here by contacting admin. miniBUDDY will publish users stories here.

Comics

miniBUDDY is here for providing lots of comics of super heros, marvel, DC etc etc! Users can read comics for free . It is just the begining... we will upload here latest comics. Follow us on social media for latest updates. Thank you (^_^) .

Cartoon Animation

miniBUDDY is cool site for kids. Users can watch lots of cartoon movies here online. And they can download their favourite movies for free. Our App on android platform will release soon. Use Brave Browser for less data consume.
